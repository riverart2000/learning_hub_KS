# Privacy Policy for Learning Hub

**Last updated: October 29, 2025**

## Introduction

Learning Hub ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Name and Email Address**: Collected during account creation for user identification and progress tracking
- **Profile Photo**: Optional image you choose to upload for your profile
- **Learning Progress Data**: Quiz scores, study progress, and completion status

### Device Information
- **Device Type**: Android device information for app optimization
- **App Usage Data**: Features used, time spent in app, and performance metrics
- **Crash Reports**: Anonymous technical data to improve app stability

### Data Storage
- **Local Storage**: All data is primarily stored locally on your device using secure local databases
- **Cloud Sync**: Basic user profile and progress data may be synced across devices (optional)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Learning Hub service
- Track your learning progress and achievements
- Sync your data across devices (when enabled)
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Provide customer support
- Send important app updates and notifications

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Track you across other websites or apps
- Use your data for marketing purposes outside the app

### We May Share Data Only When:
- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary to protect our rights or safety
- With your explicit consent
- In case of app transfer or acquisition (with notice)

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- **Local Encryption**: Data stored on your device is encrypted
- **Secure Transmission**: Data transmission uses industry-standard encryption
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal data
- **Regular Updates**: Security measures are regularly updated

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View your personal data stored in the app
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and associated data
- **Export**: Export your learning progress data
- **Opt-out**: Disable data sync and analytics

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained until you delete your account
- **Learning Progress**: Stored locally and can be synced across devices
- **Analytics Data**: Anonymized data may be retained for app improvement
- **Support Data**: Customer support communications retained for 2 years

## Children's Privacy

Learning Hub is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase**: For app analytics and crash reporting (Google)
- **Google Play Services**: For app functionality and updates
- **Device Storage**: Local device storage for data persistence

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
